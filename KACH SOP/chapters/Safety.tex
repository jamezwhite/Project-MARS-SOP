\subsection{Lost Communication (Lost Link)}
    \paragraph{Situation}
        Lost Link refers to the loss of \ac{c2} between the \ac{gcs} and the \ac{suas} during flight. This includes primary and backup telemetry, command uplink, or video downlink loss beyond a pre-established threshold. Lost link may be temporary or permanent and can occur in both \ac{vlos} and \ac{bvlos} operations. All aircraft participating in Project \ac{mars} flights must be pre-programmed with an appropriate Lost Link procedure and contingency route.

    \paragraph{Detection and Initial Assessment}
        Lost Link is detected when:
            \subparagraph{} The \ac{gcs} indicates telemetry or control signal interruption (e.g., "No RC Signal," "Lost Telemetry").
            \subparagraph{} The aircraft ceases to respond to manual inputs.
            \subparagraph{} A pre-set timeout value (e.g., 3 seconds for control link, 10 seconds for telemetry) is exceeded without recovery.
\\
        \subparagraph*{}The Operator shall immediately verify the condition by confirming: \setcounter{subparagraph}{0}
            \subparagraph{} Power and antenna integrity at the GCS.
            \subparagraph{} System status on secondary displays or via secondary control links (if available).
            \subparagraph{} Possible sources of local interference or terrain obstruction.

    \paragraph{Priorities}
        \subparagraph{} Ensure safety of personnel, airspace users, and  infrastructure.
        \subparagraph{} Prevent airspace incursion outside the authorized corridor.
        \subparagraph{} Recover the aircraft safely to a pre-designated Rally Point or \ac{rth} location.
        \subparagraph{} Maintain accountability of payloads (especially medical cargo).
        \subparagraph{} Comply with \ac{faa}, \ac{dod}, and \ac{kach} safety policies.
    
    \paragraph{Immediate Actions (All Flights)}
        \subparagraph{} Attempt manual link recovery within 30 seconds.
        \subparagraph{} If unsuccessful, monitor aircraft telemetry (if still available) and observe for programmed Lost Link behavior:
            \subparagraph{} Autonomous Return-to-Home (RTH)
            \subparagraph{} Loiter-and-land
            \subparagraph{} Pre-programmed contingency route
        \subparagraph{} Notify XXXXX.
        \subparagraph{} If aircraft is non-responsive and on unsafe trajectory, initiate termination protocol.
        \subparagraph{} Log time, location, telemetry status, and any deviation from planned route.
